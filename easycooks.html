About the Project-Easy Cooks

Worked on an Android Application during my second semester at Syracuse University. The app Messy Custard is an effective Food Delivery App.
An application that gives users the option to order deserts online. The users are given a wide range of desert options, with multiple topping options.
Database used was Firebase. Incorporated concepts such as Navigation Drawer, Recycler View, Youtube Maps, Coordinator Layout and animations.
